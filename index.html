Frequently Asked Questions & Guide

What is a YouTube Playlist Length Calculator?

A YouTube Playlist Length Calculator is an online tool that helps you calculate the total running time or duration of any public YouTube playlist. It analyzes each video, adds up their lengths, and displays the total watch time in a user-friendly format (days, hours, minutes, and seconds).

How do I calculate the total duration of a YouTube playlist?

Simply copy the link of the YouTube playlist from your browser or app, paste it into our input box in the sidebar, and click Calculate Length. The application will instantly load the videos, display their details, and show the total duration and average length.

Can I calculate playlist length at 1.25x, 1.5x, or 2x speed?

Yes, absolutely! Our tool features a dedicated Playback Speeds panel that automatically computes the total duration if you watch the playlist at faster speeds (1.25x, 1.5x, 1.75x, and 2.0x). It also displays exactly how much watch time you will save.

Do I need a Google YouTube API key to use this calculator?

No. While other online playlist calculators force you to set up Google Cloud Developer accounts and create an API Key, our tool runs a secure Node.js proxy server. This server retrieves the playlist details directly and securely on your behalf, so you can calculate lengths with zero setup.

Can I exclude specific videos from the calculation?

Yes! Our tool provides a fully interactive checkbox next to each video in the list. You can uncheck any video to exclude it, and the Total Duration, Selected Duration, average length, and speed calculations will update in real-time.

How does sorting help in calculating playlist duration?

We provide advanced sorting options (Default Order, Shortest First, Longest First, and Alphabetical). If you want to skip the longest videos to save time, you can sort by **Longest First**, deselect them, and see the updated duration instantly.
